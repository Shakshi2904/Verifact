About
Welcome to Verifacts
At Verifacts, we're on a mission to combat fake news and promote media literacy. Our advanced model predicts the credibility of news articles and sources, helping you make informed decisions in today's information-rich world.
The Need for Truth
In the age of misinformation, verifying facts is crucial. Verifacts equips you with a reliable tool to identify fake news, guiding you in separating fact from fiction.
How VeriFacts Works
Paste text, and our model assesses the authenticity of a news article. We're passionate about promoting media literacy and critical thinking to empower you.
Privacy and Ethics
Your privacy is our priority. We don't store user data, focusing solely on your quest for truth and information.
Join the Truth-Seekers
Join our community of truth-seekers, from concerned individuals to journalists and educators. Explore our blog for insights into fake news, media literacy, and the digital information landscape.
